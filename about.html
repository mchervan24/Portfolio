About

Ervan is a graphic designer residing and studying in Madiun, Indonesia. His fascination with graphic design began during his vocational school days, where he joined the design extracurricular program and learned the fundamentals of graphic design. Ervan is particularly drawn to the fusion of creativity and technology in graphic design, yielding unique artistic creations.

Ervan believes that captivating graphic design possesses the ability to capture attention and evoke emotions. Through harmonious combinations of color, shape, and composition, graphic design effectively conveys messages with precision and efficiency. Consequently, graphic design becomes a vital visual communication tool in various fields, including advertising, marketing, and art.
